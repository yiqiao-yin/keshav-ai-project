The main goals of the organization are to help young people from BIPOC and LGBTQIA+ communities gain important art and business skills that can lead to job opportunities, support youth by providing job training that understands their life experiences and helps them develop skills for earning income, assist youth in achieving their personal and professional goals in life, and teach youth about managing money and understanding finances, especially for those who come from disadvantaged families with a different understanding of money.
