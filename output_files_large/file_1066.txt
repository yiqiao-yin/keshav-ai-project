### Human:
What are the benefits of tiny houses?

### Assistant:
Tiny houses are quick, inexpensive, and easy to assemble, movable and adaptable, compatible with the Habitat for Humanity community build model, and replicable across location and need.

### Human:
What type of shelter does the Tiny House Empowerment Village operate as?

### Assistant:
The Tiny House Empowerment Village operates as a transitional-aged youth (TAY) emergency shelter following guidance provided by the City of Oakland's emergency shelter ordinance and Oakland's "community cabins" program.
