### Human:
What are the conditions that individuals or families under the second definition of homelessness must meet?

### Assistant:
Individuals or families under the second definition of homelessness must meet the following three conditions:
1. Residence will be lost within 14 days of the date of application for homeless assistance.
2. No subsequent residence has been identified.
3. The individual or family lacks the resources or support networks needed to obtain other permanent housing.

### Human:
What is the third definition of a "literally" homeless individual or family?

### Assistant:
The third definition of a "literally" homeless individual or family includes unaccompanied youth under 25 years of age or families with Category 3 children and youth who do not otherwise qualify as homeless under the previous definitions.
