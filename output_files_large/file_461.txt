### Human: What features are included in the completed Tiny House Village?
### Assistant: The completed Tiny House Village features 26 secure 8′ x 10′ Tiny Houses with murphy beds, windows, skylight, storage, electricity, furnishings, heated floors. It also includes on-site communal bathrooms and showers, a kitchen yurt for residents to cook weekly communal meals and store their own food securely, a community gathering space for meetings, and on-site Resident Assistants who live in the community.
