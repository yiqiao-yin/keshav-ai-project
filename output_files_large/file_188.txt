### Human:
What are the eligibility criteria for the Tiny House Empowerment Village transitional housing program?

### Assistant:
To be eligible for the Tiny House Empowerment Village transitional housing program, you must be between the ages of 18-23, identify either Oakland or Berkeley as your home city, and be currently "literally" homeless according to HomeStretch standards. "Literally" homeless individuals are those who lack a fixed, regular, and adequate nighttime residence.
