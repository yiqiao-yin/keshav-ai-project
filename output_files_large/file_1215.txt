### Human:
What are residents accountable to in the program?

### Assistant:
Residents are accountable both to the program and to each other for helping all community members in achieving their goals.

### Human:
For what reasons does YSA track progress toward key goals and objectives?

### Assistant:
YSA tracks progress toward key goals and objectives for several reasons, such as helping youth develop and solidify job-ready skills, improving mental and physical health and wellness, setting and meeting educational and vocational goals, and progressing toward sustainable independent living.
