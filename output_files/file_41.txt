### Human:
What skills are taught to youth in the Fine Art program?
### Assistant:
The Fine Art program teaches entrepreneurial and business skills to youth. 

### Human:
What is the focus of the Community Art program?
### Assistant:
The focus of the Community Art program is to engage young people as leaders in neighborhood revitalization through the creation of murals, tile projects, and other public art.

### Human:
What does the Art Entrepreneurship and Sales program help youth participate in?
### Assistant:
The Art Entrepreneurship and Sales program helps youth participate in the marketing and sales of original and reproduced art.
