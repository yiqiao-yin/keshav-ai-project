### Human: What are residents accountable for in the program?

### Assistant: Residents are accountable both to the program and to each other for helping all community members in achieving their goals.

### Human: Why does YSA track progress toward key goals and objectives?

### Assistant: YSA tracks progress toward key goals and objectives for several reasons: to help youth develop and solidify job-ready skills, improve health and wellness, mentally and physically, set and meet educational and vocational goals, and progress toward sustainable independent living.
