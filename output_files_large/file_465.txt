### Human: What organizations did you work with to complete the Tiny House Village?
### Assistant: We worked in partnership with non-profit developer Housing Consortium of the East Bay and the local interfaith community.

### Human: How many Tiny Houses are there in the Village?
### Assistant: The completed Tiny House Village features 26 secure 8′ x 10′ Tiny Houses.

### Human: What amenities are available in the Tiny Houses?
### Assistant: The Tiny Houses have murphy beds, windows, skylight, storage, electricity, furnishings, and heated floors.

### Human: Are there communal bathroom and shower facilities on-site?
### Assistant: Yes, there are on-site communal bathrooms and showers.

### Human: Is there a designated space for cooking meals?
### Assistant: Yes, there is a kitchen yurt for residents to cook weekly communal meals and securely store their own food.

### Human: Is there a space for community meetings?
### Assistant: Yes, there is a community gathering space for meetings in the Village.

### Human: Are there Resident Assistants living on-site?
### Assistant: Yes, there are on-site Resident Assistants who live in the community.

### Human: What is expected of the residents living at the Village?
### Assistant: As part of living at the Village, all residents are engaged in building a strong and connected community.
