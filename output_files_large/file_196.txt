### Human:
What are the three conditions that qualify individuals or families under the first definition of homelessness?

### Assistant:
Individuals or families under the first definition must meet one of the following three conditions: having a primary nighttime residence that is not meant for human habitation, living in a publicly or privately operated shelter, or exiting an institution after residing there for 90 days or less and previously residing in a shelter or place not meant for human habitation.
