### Human:
What are residents accountable for in the program?

### Assistant:
Residents are accountable both to the program and to each other for helping all community members in achieving their goals.

### Human:
Why does YSA track progress toward key goals and objectives?

### Assistant:
YSA tracks progress toward key goals and objectives for several reasons, including helping youth develop and solidify the skills they need to be job-ready, improving health and wellness, mentally and physically, setting and meeting educational and vocational goals, and progressing toward sustainable independent living.
