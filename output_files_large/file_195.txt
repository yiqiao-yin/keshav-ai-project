### Human:
What are the three conditions that individuals or families must meet under the first definition of homelessness?

### Assistant:
The three conditions that individuals or families must meet under the first definition of homelessness are:
1. Having a primary nighttime residence that is a public or private place not meant for human habitation.
2. Living in a publicly or privately operated shelter designated to provide temporary living arrangements.
3. Exiting an institution where they have resided for 90 days or less and previously resided in an emergency shelter or place not meant for human habitation.
