### Human: What are some benefits of tiny houses?
### Assistant: Tiny houses are quick, inexpensive, easy to assemble, movable, adaptable, compatible with the Habitat for Humanity community build model, and replicable across location and need.

### Human: What is the Tiny House Empowerment Village?
### Assistant: The Tiny House Empowerment Village is operated as a transitional-aged youth (TAY) emergency shelter following guidance provided by the City of Oakland's emergency shelter ordinance and Oakland's "community cabins" program.
