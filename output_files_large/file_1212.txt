### Human: What are residents accountable for in the program?

### Assistant: Residents are accountable both to the program and to each other for helping all community members in achieving their goals.

### Human: What is the purpose of YSA tracking progress toward key goals and objectives?

### Assistant: YSA tracks progress toward key goals and objectives to help youth develop and solidify the skills they need to be job-ready, improve health and wellness, mentally and physically, and set and meet educational and vocational goals, as well as progress toward sustainable independent living.
