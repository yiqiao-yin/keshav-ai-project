### Human: What are the conditions for individuals and families under the third definition of homelessness?

### Assistant: Individuals and families under the third definition must meet four conditions: being defined as homeless under other federal statutes, not having had permanent housing for 60 days prior to the homeless assistance application, experiencing persistent instability with two or more moves in the preceding 60 days, and being expected to continue in such status for an extended period due to special needs or barriers.
